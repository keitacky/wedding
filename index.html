Our Moments

Interspace Caverns

Our first trip outside of DFW. I went with him on his business trip to Austin for the first time and so I suggested we explore the Caverns. I felt a little bad since he was really too tall to be in those short spaces but he seemed to be happy to share the experience with me anyway.

San Diego Shore

Our first trip to California and our first plane ride together. The first place I wanted to go was the beach. It was so peaceful to sit and watch the ocean with Kei. I even saw a dolphin jump out of the water. This is one of my favorite photos of us.

San Diego Zoo

During the same California trip Kei took me to the zoo. I mentioned to him how much I love them and so he said he would take me to the best. This photo is us on the zip line cart that goes over the park.

Meeting Kei's Family

I was so nervous and just wanted his family to approve of me. They are the kindest and most welcoming people. I had nothing to be nervous about. I was surprised to see that Kei looks exactly like his dad.

Taiwan

Here we are in Taiwan. Kei and I don't remember what the place was called. But I enjoyed learning about the history of Taiwan.

Forever Love

Here is us again in Taiwan. We both wrote our wishes on a red ballon lantern. Kei refused to tell me what he wrote. Months later a friend told me it translates to "forever love". My wish was to learn Japanese. I do not speak Japanese. But if only one wish can come true his is much better.

Temple in Japan

I don't remember the temples name. The view was amazing and I'm so glad Kei took me here. He said the last time he visited he was a kid on a school trip.

Red Leaves in Japan

I was so excited to see the red and orange leaves. Kei wasn't sure of the timing but I still hoped and was not disappointed.

Austin Drinking

This was the night before Kei proposed to me. He took me to Austin to celebrate our 1 year anniversary. We went to our old favorite spots and some new spots. The next day we stayed the night at a beautiful resort where he proposed to me by a campfire. The weather was perfect and so was the moment.

Pumpkin Nights

Not being able to get tickets the year before, Kei made sure to get them early. We spent so long trying to get the perfect photo in front of the dragon. I was born year of the dragon so I had to get this photo right. Kei doesn't believe in that stuff but he humors me anyway.

Making Our Heart

This photo was also at the event Pumpkin Nights. We saw the heart photo opportunity and knew we had to get a photo, even if it meant waiting in line for 15 min... It was his idea for us to hold hands and make the heart. What a romantic man I'm marrying.

Arcade After Dinner

Kei and I went to a new restaurant in our area. It had started to pour heavy rain when we wanted to leave. He went to his car to bring me the rain jacket I'm wearing in the photo. He also brought an umbrella and held it for us on our way to the nearby arcade.

New Years

Here we got to celebrate with our friends and have the perfect New Years kiss. His friend group has been so accepting of me and I feel as though they are truly my friends as well.

Playing In Snow

After a stressful day of wedding dress shopping I was feeling scared I wouldn't find a dress I loved. Kei took me to play in the snow and forget about my worries for a while.

Valentines Day

Kei took me to Heritage House. A beautiful restaurant in a repurposed old southern house that only serves food "farm to table". I spent the week before learning how to walk in 5 in. heels for my outfit and ended up biting off more than I could chew. Kei gave me his arm for support and made sure I was steady on my feet.
